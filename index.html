Oreos

Oreo cookies, the best late night snack!

They're delicious, made with love, and-really, really yummy!

Oreos are a beloved sandwich cookies consisting of two chocolate wafers with a sweet cream filling, first introduced by Nabisco in 1912. Known for its iconic design and the phrase "Twist, Lick, Dunk," Oreo has become one of the best-selling cookies worldwide. While the classic version features chocolate and vanilla flavors, Oreos come in a variety of limited-edition and international flavors, such as mint, peanut butter, and birthday cake. Oreo's unique combination of crisp cookies and creamy filling, along with its versatile use in desserts, has made it a staple snack in many households.
